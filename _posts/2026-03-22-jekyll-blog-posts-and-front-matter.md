Jekyll uses specially named files and front matter to create blog posts.
The files must be named **_posts/YYYY-MM-DD-title.md** and must include _title_ and _date_ in the front matter.

> **Note:**
  If your blog post date doesn't follow the correct date convention, you'll receive an error and your site won't build.
  Also, ensure that there are hyphens (-) between the title's words.
